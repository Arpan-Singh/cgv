What We Offer

Comprehensive Hospitality Consulting Services

From single-outlet cafés to multi-property hotel groups, we have the expertise to set up, optimize and scale any hospitality operation.

🍽️

Menu Engineering

Our menu engineers combine sales data analysis, food cost modelling, and consumer psychology to redesign your menu for maximum profitability. We use the BCG matrix framework to classify dishes as Stars, Plowhorses, Puzzles, or Dogs — and develop a strategy for each category.

Sales Mix AnalysisIdentify top & bottom performers
Food Cost AuditRecipe-level cost breakdown

🏗️

Kitchen Setup Consulting

We design commercial kitchens that work — not just look impressive on paper. Our ergonomic flow analysis minimizes steps, maximizes output, and meets all FSSAI and safety requirements. Every project includes equipment specifications, vendor shortlisting, and site supervision during installation.

CAD Layout Design Equipment Specs Vendor Sourcing FSSAI Compliance Site Supervision Commissioning
📑

SOP Development

Detailed, illustrated standard operating procedures that make your operations teachable, measurable, and scalable. We cover every function — from opening and closing checklists to food safety protocols, complaint handling, and inventory control. Our SOPs are written for the actual people who will use them.

📊

Cost Optimization

We conduct a comprehensive profit-and-loss audit to identify leakage points across your supply chain, kitchen, and labour costs. Average food cost savings across our clients: 8–18%. We also benchmark your supplier pricing against market rates and help you renegotiate better terms.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out an enquiry form, contact us via email, or engage our services. This may include your name, email address, phone number, company name, city, and project details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide the consulting services you request
  • To send you a confirmation of your enquiry submission
  • To improve our website and service offerings based on feedback
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage & Security

Your enquiry data is stored securely and is only accessible to the Crescent Globe Ventures team. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.

5. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to ensure proper functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@crescentglobe.in.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@crescentglobe.in or at our West Delhi office.
